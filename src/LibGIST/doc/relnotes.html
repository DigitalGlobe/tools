libgist v.2.0/amdb v.1.0 Release Notes

Libgist

  • Node deletion is not implemented. Instead, empty nodes are simply left in the tree.
  • Bounding predicates are not shrunk when leaf items are deleted.
  • Nearest-neighbor queries (e.g., operator ~ for the spatial tree extensions) are not supported by the delete operation.

Amdb

  • The menu items "New Analysis" and "Complete Analysis" are not implemented. Use gistcmdline (commands createanl, wkldstats, splitstats and penaltystats) to perform these operations. Use amdb to view the computed metrics.
Comments, questions and suggestions may be directed to gist@postgres.berkeley.edu
